Ada Lovelace

The first computer programmer

Timeline of Ada Lovelace's life:

Please note that this timeline has the purpose of being a mise en bouche and not an exhaustive biography.

  • 1815 - Born in London, England.
  • 1833 - Is introduced to Charles Babbage by her tutor Mary Somerville, a scientist.
  • 1842-3 - Translates an article on Babbage's Analytical Engine and adds notes to it.
  • 1852 - Dies at the age of 36.

Read more about Ada Lovelace's work and life on her Wikipedia entry.
